All your calendars.

Your calendars are everywhere. Family on iCloud. Holidays from some website. Birthdays buried in your contacts. Work on the phone calendar.

iCloud

Switched to Android but your family is still on Apple? Sync with iCloud calendars directly. No workaround needed.

Quick Add

Type or say “Coffee with Kash tomorrow 3pm” and the event is created. Handles dates, times, recurrence, and locations.

Insights

See where your time goes. Busiest days, back-to-back streaks, free blocks, and more. All computed on-device.

Widgets Agenda, date, week, and month.
CalDAV Nextcloud, Radicale, Fastmail, Baikal, and others.
Device calendar Your phone's built-in calendar alongside everything else.
ICS subscriptions Public holidays, school terms, sports seasons.
Birthdays & anniversaries Pulled from your contacts automatically.
Local Works fully offline out of the box.

Private by default
