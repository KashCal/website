Also in the app

Quick Add
Type "Coffee with Kash tomorrow 3pm" and the event gets created. Handles dates, times, recurrence, and locations.
CalDAV sync
Native sync with Nextcloud, Radicale, Fastmail, Baikal, Zoho, SoGo, Stalwart, and others. No middleware, no relay.
Device calendar
Events from the calendars already on your phone show up alongside everything else. That includes your Google Calendar and Outlook accounts, synced by Android's own calendar system.
ICS subscriptions
Subscribe to any ICS URL. Public holidays, school terms, sports seasons. Refresh interval is yours to set.
Widgets
Agenda, date, week, and month. Check your schedule without opening the app. Material You palette, so they match the rest of your home screen.
Birthdays and anniversaries
Pulled from your contacts automatically. Shown in the calendar like any other recurring event.
Works offline
Don't want to sync anything? KashCal works fully offline from first install. Add a sync account later if you change your mind.

What doesn't cost you

Free. Not a trial, not a freemium tier, not a pro version locked behind a paywall. Open source under Apache 2.0. No ads, no trackers, no analytics, no KashCal account. Credentials are encrypted with the Android Keystore. Other apps can't read your events. Speaks 67 languages.
